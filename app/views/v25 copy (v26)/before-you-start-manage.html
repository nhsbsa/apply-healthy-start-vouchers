Before you start

We use NHS and government data sources to find and update your claim.

Checking your identity

We need to check your identity to keep your claim secure. You’ll need to enter:

  • your National Insurance number
  • a security code that we’ll send to the mobile phone number you gave us when you applied for Healthy Start

GOV.UK can help you find your National Insurance number if you do not remember it.

If you cannot find your National Insurance number, or did not give us your mobile phone number when you first applied, you’ll need to contact us to update your claim.

What we’ll ask for

When we’ve confirmed your identity, we can update your claim. We’ll ask for:

  • your estimated due date if you’re pregnant
  • the name and date of birth of any children under 4 you’d like to add
Information:

Make sure you enter any children’s details exactly as they appear on your Universal Credit claim.
