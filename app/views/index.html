Healthy Start online journeyThe prototypes

The Healthy Start scheme helps eligible applicants with the cost of buying food and milk. These are the prototypes of the Healthy Start ‘Apply’ and 'Existing Claims' journeys. The aim of the prototypes is to make it as easy as possible for people to apply online and find out if they can get Healthy Start payments.
