Get help to buy food and milk - (Healthy Start)

If you're more than 10 weeks pregnant or have a child under 4 years old, you could get help to buy fruit, vegetables and milk.

If you qualify you'll get:

  • £4.25 each week of your pregnancy from the tenth week
  • £8.50 each week for children from birth to 1 year old
  • £4.25 each week for children between 1 and 4 years old
Information:

The payments will stop when your child is 4 years old or if your eligibility changes, for example if there's a change to your income or benefits.
