Terms and conditions

You must tell us if your circumstances or personal details change.

You understand that you must only buy permitted items with the money you’re given.

You understand that we’re acting on behalf of the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC).

You confirm that you:

  • give us permission to submit this application on your behalf
  • know we’ll use your information and share it with other organisations where necessary
  • understand your data will not be transferred outside the European Economic Area and will be delete within 24 months after your eligibility ends
  • know this is a pilot scheme and consent to your data being used to evaluate it

You understand and agree that the DHSC can:

  • check with other organisations associated with the service that the information you’ve given is correct
  • stop your payments and ask you to repay the money if you apply illegally
