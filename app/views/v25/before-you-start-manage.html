Before you start

We use NHS and government data sources to find and update your claim.

Important: National Insurance number

You need your National Insurance number to update your claim online.

GOV.UK can help you find your National Insurance number if you do not remember it.

If you cannot find your National Insurance number, you’ll need to contact us to update your claim.

We’ll use your National Insurance number to find your claim. We’ll also ask for your:

  • estimated due date if you’re adding a new pregnancy
  • child’s name and date of birth if you’re adding a new child – these must exactly match their details on your Universal Credit account
