Before you start

We use NHS and government data sources to find and update your claim.

Important: Confirming your identity

We use your National Insurance number to confirm your identity

If you do not know this, we’ll ask for other personal details, including your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • postcode

When we’ve confirmed your identity, we’ll ask for:

  • your estimated due date if you’re adding a new pregnancy
  • the names and dates of birth of any children under 4 you’d like to add to your claim
Information:

Universal Credit claimants

To add a new child, the name and date of birth you give us must exactly match their details on your Universal Credit claim.

Go to your Universal Credit account to check how their details are listed before you update your claim.
