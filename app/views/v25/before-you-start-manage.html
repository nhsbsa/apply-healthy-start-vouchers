Before you start

We use NHS and government data sources to find and update your Healthy Start claim. You can make changes and add new details, including a new pregnancy or child under 4.

You will only be able to view the new information you give us. You cannot use this service to check any existing details about pregnancies, children or payments.

Important: What you’ll need

We need to check your identity to keep your claim secure. To update your details, you’ll need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • mobile phone – we need to send you a security code by text message

GOV.UK can help you find your National Insurance number if you do not remember it.

If you cannot find your National Insurance number or you do not have a mobile number linked to your Healthy Start account, you’ll need to contact us to update your claim.

What we’ll ask for

When we’ve confirmed your identity, you can tell us what update you’d like to make. We’ll ask for:

  • your estimated due date if you’re pregnant
  • the name and date of birth of any children under 4 you’d like to add
Information:

Make sure you enter any children’s details exactly as they appear on your Universal Credit claim.
