How it works

NHS Healthy Start payments help you with the cost of healthy food and milk during pregnancy and while your children are under the age of 4.

If you’re eligible, you’ll get a Healthy Start card, and we’ll make payments onto your card every 4 weeks. The payment is made up of:

  • £4.25 for each week of your pregnancy from the tenth week
  • £8.50 each week for children from birth to 1 year old
  • £4.25 each week for children between 1 and 4 years old

You can use the card in most shops that sell healthy food and milk.

You can use your card to collect:

  • free vitamins to keep you healthy while you’re pregnant or breastfeeding
  • free vitamin drops for children from birth to 4 years old
Information:

If you live in Scotland

You may be able to get help from Best Start Foods (opens in a new tab) instead.
