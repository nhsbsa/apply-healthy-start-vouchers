Update your details

If you get Healthy Start payments, you need to let us know about any changes in your circumstances.

This helps us make sure you’re being paid correctly.

Update your details online

You can update your Healthy Start claim online if you need to:

  • add a new pregnancy
  • let us know you’ve given birth and add your new baby to your claim
  • add a new child under 4

What you'll need

Before you start, check you’ve got your:

  • National Insurance number
  • mobile phone – we'll send you a 6 digit security code by text message to keep your details secure
  • estimated due date if you’re adding a new pregnancy
  • child’s name and date of birth if you’re adding a new child

Important: Universal Credit claimants

We cannot start making Healthy Start payments for a child until you’ve added them to your Universal Credit claim.

Before you add any new children, including newborn babies, to your Healthy Start claim, go to your Universal Credit account to check they’re named on your claim.

Update your details by phone or email

If you need to:

  • change your name or address
  • change the name of any children on your claim
  • stop your payments for a pregnancy or a child

You can contact us by:

Information:

Email: healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

Phone: 0300 330 7010
Phone lines are open 8am to 6pm, Monday to Friday (except public holidays).

Find out more about call charges (opens in a new tab).

If your card has been lost, damaged or stolen

Call 0300 330 2090 if your card has been lost, damaged or stolen. When you call, you’ll need your:

  • date of birth
  • 10 digit client ID – you can find this on any letters, emails or text messages we’ve sent you

This is an automated phone line that’s available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

If you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss

Our automated phone line is not accessible if you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss or tinnitus.

You can call us on 0300 330 7010 and use the:

  • Relay app (opens in a new tab)
  • Relay textphone service (opens in a new tab)

This phone line is open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (except public holidays).
