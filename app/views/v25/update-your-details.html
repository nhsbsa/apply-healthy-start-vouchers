Update your details

You can use our online service to:

  • add a new pregnancy
  • add a new baby or child
  • replace a lost, damaged or stolen card
  • update your personal details, such as your name or address
  • let us know if you were pregnant and you’ve lost your baby
  • let us know if you are no longer the main carer for a child on your claim
What is a main carer?

We’ll make payments to the child’s main carer.

A child’s main carer:

  • has parental responsibility for the child
  • is the person the child lives with most of the time

We can only make one payment per child. In some circumstances, you’ll need to decide which parent will apply for Healthy Start payments as the main carer. For example, if you:

  • both live in the same house as your child
  • live apart but your child spends an equal amount of time with both parents (sometimes known as 50-50 custody)

If you get Healthy Start payments, but you are no longer the main carer for your child, you should let us know.

What we’ll ask you for

If we need to send you a new card, we’ll ask you for your:

  • date of birth
  • client ID – you can find this on any notifications we’ve sent you

To make any other updates to your claim online, you'll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • a security code - we’ll send you this by text message if there’s a mobile number linked to your Healthy Start claim
Information:

We can only add new children if you enter their details exactly as they’re listed on your Universal Credit claim.
