We cannot find your Healthy Start claim as we cannot match your details with the information you’ve given DWP or HMRC.

Follow these steps to make sure you’re entering your details correctly.

  1. Check how your details are listed on your Universal Credit account (opens in a new tab) or Child Tax Credit letter.
  2. Go back and update your details again - make sure you enter your personal information exactly as it appears on your Universal Credit or Child Tax Credit statement.

If we still cannot update your Healthy Start claim online after you’ve checked your details match, you should contact us.

If you’re applying for Healthy Start for the first time, you’ll need to go back and start a new application.
