We cannot find your claim

We need your National Insurance number

We need your National Insurance number so we can find your claim.

You can find your National Insurance number on your:

  • National Insurance card or letter
  • Universal Credit statement
  • benefit award notice
  • payslip
  • P60

GOV.UK have more advice on how you can find a lost National Insurance number (opens in a new tab)

If you find your National Insurance number, you can go back and update your Healthy Start claim online.

If you cannot find your National Insurance number

If you cannot find your National Insurance number, you need to contact us.

We’ll ask for some personal details so we can find and update your claim. You’ll need to tell us your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • address, including your postcode
  • estimated due date if you’re adding a new pregnancy
  • child’s name and date of birth if you’re adding a new child

Make sure the details you give us for yourself and any children under 4 exactly match the details on your Universal Credit account (opens in a new tab).

You can contact us by:

Information:

Email: healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

We aim to respond within 2 working days
