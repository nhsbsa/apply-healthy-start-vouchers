Contact us

You need to email or call us to update your Healthy Start claim

You need to contact us to update your Healthy Start claim, including if you need to:

  • update your personal details – for example your name or address
  • update your children’s details
  • tell us about any other changes in your circumstances
  • ask a question about your Healthy Start claim

You can contact us by:

Information:

Email: healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

Telephone: 0300 330 7010

Phone lines are open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (except public holidays).

Find out more about call charges (opens in new tab).

If your card has been lost, damaged or stolen

Call 0300 330 2090 to report your card as lost, damaged or stolen

You’ll need your date of birth and your client ID which you can find on any notifications we’ve sent you.

This is an automated phone line that’s available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

If you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss

Our automated phone line is not accessible if you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss or tinnitus.

You can contact us by calling 0300 330 7010 and using the:

  • Relay app (opens in a new tab)
  • Relay textphone service (opens in a new tab)

This phone line is open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (excluding public holidays).

What we’ll ask you for

We’ll ask for your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth

If you’re calling to ask a question about your Healthy Start card, you’ll need to confirm one of the following:

  • the place where you last used your card
  • the date you last used your card
  • how much you spent the last time you used your card
