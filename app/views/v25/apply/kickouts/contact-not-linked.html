Call us on 0300 330 7010

We cannot update your Healthy Start claim online

To update your Healthy Start claim online, we need to send you a security code.

We cannot find the information we need to send your security code.

If you call 0300 330 7010, we can update your claim. You’ll need to tell us your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • estimated due date if you’re adding a pregnancy
  • child's name and date of birth if you’re adding a new child

If you’re adding a new child under 4, make sure you enter their name and date of birth exactly as they appear on your Universal Credit claim (opens in a new tab) or Child Tax Credit letter.

Phone lines are open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (expect public holidays).

Find out more about call charges (opens in a new tab).

If your card has been lost, damaged or stolen

Call 0300 330 2090 to report your card as lost, damaged or stolen.

You’ll need to give us your date of birth and your client ID which you can find on any notifications we’ve sent you.

This is an automated phone line that’s available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

If you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss

Our automated phone line is not accessible if you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss or tinnitus.

You can contact us by calling 0300 330 7010 and using the:

  • Relay app (opens in a new tab)
  • Relay textphone service (opens in a new tab)

This phone line is open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (excluding public holidays).
