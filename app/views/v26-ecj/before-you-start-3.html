Before you start

The Get help to buy food and milk (Healthy Start) service uses government data sources to check your eligibility.

We will ask for:

  • your personal details
  • your pregnancy due date (if you are pregnant)
  • your children's details

If we are unable to confirm your eligibility, we might ask you to send us:

  • proof of your identity (birth certificate, marriage certificate, passport, driving licence or immigration visa)
  • proof of your eligibility (benefit awards notice)
  • proof of your children's eligibility (birth certificate)

Would you like to manage your Healthy Start card online?

If you would, you need to provide your email address as part of your application. Without your email address we will not be able to create an online account for you. It would also be benifical to provide a mobile number as an alternative method of validation, however this is optional.

Important: Universal Credit claimants

To ensure you are paid correctly for all your children, make sure each child is named on your Universal Credit claim.

Go to your Universal Credit account to check this.
