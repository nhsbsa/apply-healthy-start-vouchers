Uploading the photo

If you took the photo on the same device you're using for your application, you can return to your application now and upload it.

If you used a different device (eg. a smartphone)

You'll need to transfer the photo to the device you're using to apply (eg. a laptop).

You can do this by:

  • sending it via Bluetooth®
  • attaching a cable between the two devices
