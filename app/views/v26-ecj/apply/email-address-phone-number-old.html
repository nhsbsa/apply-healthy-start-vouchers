Get updates about your claim by email

After your card arrives in the post, the quickets and easiest way to get information about your claim is by email. We can email you:
• when we've made a payment onto your card
• if your payments are changing
• reminders about keeping your details up to date
If you do not have an email address, we can send you information by text message.
or

If you do not want to recieve information by email or text message

If you do not give us an email address or mobile phone number, we'll send you information about your claim by post. It takes longer to get information by post than by email or text message.
