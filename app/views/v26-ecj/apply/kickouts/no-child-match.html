You are not eligible for Healthy Start

To be eligible for Healthy Start, you must be more than 10 weeks pregnant or have at least one child under 4 years old.

If you’re applying for any children under 4, make sure you’ve entered your children’s details exactly as they appear on your Universal Credit claim. You should check their:

  • full names
  • dates of birth

If you’re more than 10 weeks pregnant, check you’ve entered your estimated due date correctly.

If you’re less than 10 weeks pregnant and have no other children under 4, you can apply again after your tenth week of pregnancy.
