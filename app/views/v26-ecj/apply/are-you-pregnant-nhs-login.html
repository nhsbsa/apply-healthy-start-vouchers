Are you more than 10 weeks pregnant?

You must be more than 10 weeks pregnant to add a new pregnancy to your claim.
If you are less than 10 weeks pregnant

If you are less than 10 weeks pregnant and have children under 4 years old, you can add your pregnancy onto your application when you’ve completed your 10th week of pregnancy by:

  • emailing healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • calling 0300 330 7010 – charges may apply

Find out more about call charges (opens in a new tab).

If you are less than 10 weeks pregnant and have no other children under 4 years old, you’ll need to apply again when you’ve completed your 10th week of pregnancy.
