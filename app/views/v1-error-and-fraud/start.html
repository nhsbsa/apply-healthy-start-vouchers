Get help to buy food and milk (Healthy Start)

Use this service to apply for Healthy Start card with money on it to buy food and milk.

Who can use this service

You can use this service if you or a partner you live with:

  • are more than 10 weeks pregnant
  • have a child under 4 years old
  • claim Universal Credit
  • earn less than £408 a month

The card will be topped up every 4 weeks with payments of:

  • £4.25 each week of your pregnancy from 10 week
  • £8.50 each week for children from birth to 1 year old
  • £4.25 each week for children between 1 and 4 years old

Before you start

To apply you will need to provide personal details to check you are eligible for Healthy Start.

You will need to tell us your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • email address
  • National Insurance number
  • NHS number (if you know it)
  • pregnancy due date (if you are pregnant)
  • name and date of birth for children under 4 years old

If you live with a partner you will need to provide their name, date of birth and national insurance number.

Universal Credit claimants

The details you use to apply for Healthy Start must be the same as the details on your Universal Credit claim.

Go to your Universal Credit account (opens in a new tab) to check.
