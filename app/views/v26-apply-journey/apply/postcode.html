Why we ask for your postcode

We use your postcode to find your address. This lets us match your details with the benefit agency and check you’re eligible for Healthy Start.

If you’re under 18 and do not have a current benefit award notice, you cannot apply online. You’ll need to contact us by:

  • email: healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • telephone: 0300 330 7010 - this call will be charged at the local rate
