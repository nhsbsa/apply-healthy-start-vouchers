Before you start

The Get help to buy food and milk (Healthy Start) service uses government data sources to check your eligibility.

We will ask for:

  • your personal details
  • your pregnancy due date (if you are pregnant)
  • your children's details

Important: Universal Credit claimants

To make sure you are paid correctly for all your children, make sure that each child is named on your Universal Credit claim.

Go to your Universal Credit account to check this.

If we need more information about your application, we might ask you to send us:

  • proof of your identity (birth certificate, marriage certificate, passport, driving licence or immigration visa)
  • proof of your eligibility (benefit awards notice)
  • proof of your children's eligibility (birth certificate)

If we ask to see these, make sure your documents are valid. We cannot accept expired documents.
