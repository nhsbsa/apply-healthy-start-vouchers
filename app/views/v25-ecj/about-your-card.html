Activating and managing your card

You’ll get your card by post within 5 to 10 working days. It will come with your first payment on it.

We’ll add money to your card every 4 weeks.

Your payments may become lower or stop when your child turns 4 or if there’s a change in your income or benefits.

You can call 0300 330 2090 to manage your card, including if you need to:

  • activate your card
  • get your PIN
  • check your balance

This is an automated phone line that’s available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Find out more about call charges (opens in a new tab).

If you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss

Our automated phone line is not accessible if you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss or tinnitus.

You can call us on 0300 330 7010 and use the:

  • Relay app (opens in a new tab)
  • Relay textphone service (opens in a new tab)

This phone line is open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (except public holidays).

Activating your card

To activate your card, call 0300 330 2090 with your:

  • date of birth
  • 10 digit client ID – this is printed on the top of the letter that comes with your card

Choosing your access code

When you activate your card, you’ll get to choose your telephone access code. This is a 6 digit code that you need to get PIN reminders or check your balance.

Getting your PIN

When you’ve activated your card, we’ll send you your PIN by email or text message.

If you did not give us your email address or mobile number when you applied, we’ll send your PIN by post 2 days after we send your card.

You can get a PIN reminder by calling 0300 330 2090. We’ll ask for your date of birth and 10 digit client ID which you can find on any letters, emails or text messages we’ve sent you.

Checking your balance

You can check your balance:

  • at a cashpoint using your PIN
  • by calling 0300 330 2090 - we’ll ask for your date of birth and 6 digit access code
Information:

You cannot use your Healthy Start card to withdraw cash.

Turning off contactless payments

If you do not want to make contactless payments with your Healthy Start card, you can let us know by:

  • emailing healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • calling 0300 330 7010

If you turn off contactless payments, you’ll need to enter your PIN whenever you use your Healthy Start card.

Replace a lost, damaged or stolen card

Call 0300 330 2090 if your card has been lost, damaged or stolen. When you call, you’ll need your:

  • date of birth
  • 10 digit client ID – you can find this on any letters, emails or text messages we’ve sent you

If there's any suspicious activity on your card

If you there’s any suspicious activity on your card, let us know as soon as possible by:

  • emailing healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • calling 0300 330 7010

We’ll send you a dispute form so you can give us more information. Any transactions that are made before you report your card as lost or stolen will be charged to your account.
