How to apply

If you’ve received a letter from the NHS

You can apply online. To apply, you will need your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society account details
  • baby’s due date (if you’re pregnant)

Information:

If you’ve not received a letter from the NHS, you can apply for Healthy Start vouchers (opens in a new tab) instead.
