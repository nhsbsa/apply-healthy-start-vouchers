Get help to buy food and milk - (Healthy Start)

Shopping with your card

You can use your Healthy Start card to buy healthy foods on their own or with other shopping.

You cannot spend more than the balance of the card.

If you want to make a contactless payment, the first time you use your card you’ll need to insert your card and use your PIN. After that, you can make contactless payments of up to £45.

If you're buying only healthy foods

You can pay for them using your Healthy Start card, like you would with a normal contactless bank card. This means you can pay by tapping your card on the card device at checkout. You can also use your card as a chip and PIN card if you prefer. Just put it in the card device and enter your PIN.

If you're buying healthy food with other food or drink

You can split the payment between your Healthy Start card and your normal bank card or cash. Just tell the cashier you want to do this. You'll also need to tell them how much you want to spend on your Healthy Start card and how much you want to spend on your normal bank card or in cash.

If you're buying healthy food at a self-checkout machine

You cannot split your payment between your Healthy Start card and another bank card at self-checkout machines. You can only part pay using your Healthy Start card if you're paying the rest in cash.

Do not worry if you make a mistake when using your Healthy Start card to buy healthy food with other shopping. It will not affect your benefit payments.

Refunds

If you need to get a refund on something you've bought, you can get the refund in the same way as you would with any bank card. Go back to the shop with your items, your receipt and your card and ask for a refund.

Turning off contactless payments

If you do not want your Healthy Start card to be contactless, contact us.

This will mean that you can only use your card by entering it into the card device and using your PIN.

Turning on online payments

If you cannot use your Healthy Start card in stores, or are struggling to find healthy food locally, you can call us so we can let you use your card online. To turn on online payments, contact us.

You can request to use your card to pay online if you gave us your mobile number when you applied. If you did not give us your mobile number when you applied, you can contact us.

This will mean that you can use your card to shop online. If you’re shopping online, then you may have to pay delivery charges for your shopping.

What to do when paying online

When you checkout online, enter your Healthy Start card details like you would with any normal bank card.

After you submit these details, you'll be sent a text that includes a One Time Passcode. Before you can complete your payment, you'll be asked to enter this One Time Passcode.

If you're just using your Healthy Start card to buy healthy food online, you can use it to buy everything in your basket.
