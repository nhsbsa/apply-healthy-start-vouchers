What is your address?

You should enter the address listed on your most recent benefit award notice.

Why we ask for your address

We use your address to match your details with the benefit agency and check you’re eligible for Healthy Start.

If you’re eligible, we’ll send your Healthy Start card to this address.

If you’re under 18 and do not have a current benefit award notice, you cannot apply online. You’ll need to contact us by:

  • email: healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • telephone: 0300 330 7010 - this call will be charged at the local rate
