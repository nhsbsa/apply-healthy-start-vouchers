Before you start

The Get help to buy food and milk (Healthy Start) service uses government data sources to check your eligibility.

We'll ask for your:

  • personal details (including your National Insurance number)
  • expected due date (if you're pregnant)
  • children's names and dates of birth (if you have any children under 4 years old)

Important: Universal Credit claimants

To be paid correctly for all your children, make sure that each child is named on your Universal Credit claim.

Go to your Universal Credit account to check this.
