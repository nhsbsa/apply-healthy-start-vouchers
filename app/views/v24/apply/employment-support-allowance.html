Which type of Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) do you get?

There are 2 types of ESA, Income-related and Contribution-based.

I'm not sure which type I get

Check your benefit award notice

On the last page of your benefit letter, look under the heading 'Income and Benefits'.

It will say either:

  • get an Income-related entitlement
  • are entitled to Contribution-based ESA

Find out more about the different types of ESA.
