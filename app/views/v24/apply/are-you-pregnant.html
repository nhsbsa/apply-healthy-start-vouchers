Are you more than 10 weeks pregnant?

You must have completed your 10 full weeks of pregnancy.
If you're less than 10 weeks pregnant

If you're less than 10 weeks pregnant and have children under 4 years old, you can still apply online for these children.

Once you're more than 10 weeks pregnant, tell us and we'll add your pregnancy onto your application. You can contact us by:

  • emailing healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • calling 0300 330 7010 – charges may apply

Find out more about call charges (opens in a new tab)

If you are less than 10 weeks pregnant and have no other children under 4 years old, you’ll need to apply again when you’ve completed your 10th week of pregnancy.
