Get help to buy food and milk - (Healthy Start)

Eligibility

You’ll qualify for the Healthy Start scheme if either:

  • you’re at least 10 weeks pregnant
  • you have at least one child under 4

You must also get Child Tax Credit with a household income of £16,190 or less.

Information:

You’ll also be eligible for the Healthy Start scheme if you’re pregnant and under 18, even if you do not receive any benefits.

Working Tax Credit run-on is the payment you receive for a further 4 weeks immediately after you stop qualifying for Working Tax Credit (opens in a new tab).
