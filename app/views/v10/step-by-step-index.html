Get help to buy food and milk: step by step

Check what you need to do to get help to buy food and milk.

  1. Step 1 Find out if you can get help to buy food and milk

    If you’re pregnant or have a child under 4, you could get help to buy healthy food and milk.

    1. What is the Healthy Start scheme?
    2. Check if you qualify for the Healthy Start scheme

  2. Step 2 Apply to get help to buy food and milk

    You can apply if you’ve received a letter from the NHS and get Child Tax Credit with a household income of £16,190 or less.

    1. Apply to get help to buy food and milk (Healthy Start)

  3. Step 3 How to activate your card

    You’ll receive your card by post. It’ll come with your first payment on it and instructions on how to activate and use it.

    1. How to activate your card

  4. Step 4 How to use your card

    You’re only allowed to buy certain types of milk, infant formula, fruit, vegetables and pulses.

    1. How to check your balance
    2. Check what healthy food and milk you can buy
    3. How to report a card lost, damaged or stolen

  5. Step 5 How to eat healthily

    Eating a healthy, balanced diet is an important part of maintaining good health, and can help you and your family feel your best.

    1. Check if you are getting 5 portions of fruit and vegetables every day
    2. Find recipe ideas to help you and your family stay healthy
    3. Tips to eat well for less

  6. Step 6 Report any change of circumstances

    To make sure you can still get the help you’re entitled to you must tell us about a change in your circumstances.

    1. Check what changes you need to report
