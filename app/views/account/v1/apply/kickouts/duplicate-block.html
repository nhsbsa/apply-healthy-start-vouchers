We cannot continue with your application online

Our records show you may already get Healthy Start payments.

You can only have one active Healthy Start claim at one time.

If you need to update your details

If you already get Healthy Start payments, you do not need to make a new application.

You should update your existing claim and let us know about a new:

  • pregnancy
  • child under 4 - you may have recently given birth or have a new child living with you who you’re responsible for
Information:

You can add a new pregnancy or child to your claim online.

If you cannot use the online service or you need us to stop making payments for a pregnancy or child, you can contact us.


Replacing a lost, damaged or stolen card

Call 0118 338 5810 if you need a new Healthy Start card.

You’ll need your date of birth and your client ID which you can find on any notifications we’ve sent you.

This is an automated phone line that’s 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Find out more about call charges (opens in a new tab).

If you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss

Our automated phone line is not accessible if you’re Deaf or have a hearing loss or tinnitus.

You can call us on 0300 330 7010 and use the:

  • Relay app (opens in a new tab)
  • Relay textphone service (opens in a new tab)

This phone line is open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (except public holidays).

If you think there’s been a mistake

You should contact us as soon as possible if you have never applied for Healthy Start, or you are not sure if you already get Healthy Start payments.

You can contact us by:

  • using our online form
  • calling 0300 330 7010

Phone lines are open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (except public holidays).
