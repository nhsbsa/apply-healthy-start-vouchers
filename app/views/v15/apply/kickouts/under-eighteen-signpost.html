Application unsuccessful

Your application may have been unsuccessful because you:

  • don’t qualify based on the benefits you get and your family income
  • entered something incorrectly

If you think you should qualify, make sure you enter the same details you used when you applied for any benefits.

You may still be entitled to Healthy Start

If you are under 18 years old and at least 10 weeks pregnant, you can apply for Healthy Start by downloading this application form (PDF, 100KB).

When you have filled it in you should send it back to us with a good quality copy of either your:

  • birth certificate
  • passport
  • driving licence

This can either be a scan, photocopy or photo of the original document. It will be used to confirm your age and identity.

You can send the form and the copy of your identification to us by either:

  • email to healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • post to NHS Business Services Authority, Bridge House, 152 Pilgrim Street, Newcastle upon Tyne, NE1 6SN
