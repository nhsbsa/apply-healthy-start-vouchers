Contact us

Card problems and queries

To activate your card, get your PIN, hear your balance, or to report your card as lost, damaged, or stolen, you can contact us by:

  • Telephone: 0300 330 7010
  • This phoneline is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Find out about call charges (opens in a new tab)

Any other queries

If there’s a change in your circumstances, you think a mistake has been made, or you have any other queries, you can contact us by:

  • Telephone: 0300 330 7010
  • This phoneline is available Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm (except public holidays).

Find out about call charges (opens in a new tab)

  • Email: healthy.start@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
  • We aim to respond to your email within 2 working days.

If you’re claiming benefits, you’ll also need to report a pregnancy and birth to the benefits agencies (opens in a new tab).
