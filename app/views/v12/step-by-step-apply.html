Get help to buy food and milk - (Healthy Start)

Apply to get help to buy food and milk (Healthy Start)

If you’ve received a letter from the NHS and get Child Tax Credit with a household income of £16,190 or less

You can apply online. To apply, you will need your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • baby’s due date (if you’re pregnant)

Information:

If you’ve not received a letter from the NHS

You cannot apply online yet, but you may still qualify for Healthy Start vouchers (opens in a new tab) instead.
